Dearest Priya,

Something I Want to Say to You ✨

Priya, You already know how much I like you. I don’t feel lust towards you. And I can’t even say I’m “in love” with you, because the truth is… I don’t even know what love really is right now. All I know is that I don’t lust after you, and I don’t know if this is love either. I won’t say “I’m in love with you” because I feel like those words have limits. What I feel for you goes beyond what I can describe. I’ve met and dated people before, but I’ve never felt this way about anyone. It’s not because I can’t be with someone else—it’s because, for me, you’re enough. I don’t feel like giving my time, effort, or even my money to anyone else. If I want to do something for someone, it’s you. People say that in a man’s life, there will be one woman who becomes different from everyone else. After her, everyone else is just another person he meets. That’s exactly who you are to me. Your happiness means more to me than my own. You’re like my childhood friend, my best friend, my safe place, my family—everything to me. Please don’t become a stranger as life moves forward. People might think it’s just attraction or that I’m saying all this because of my age. But it isn’t. Even I don’t know why I feel this way. I still haven’t figured it out. I just know that you mean so much to me. I can’t stay distant from you. I can’t go long without getting scolded by you either. You’re the one person whose mood decides how my entire day goes. I don’t even know if you realize that, but it’s true. Do you know how much I respect you? I’ll give you one example. I can kneel before you anywhere, anytime—not because I’m forced to, not because I’m scared of you, but out of pure respect, admiration, and affection. If you tell me something, I’ll listen. If you ask me to leave, I’ll leave. So… I’ll stop. I really miss you sometimes. I wish I could spend more time with you, but I don’t even know if that’ll ever happen. I just wanted you to know how I truly feel. Nothing more, nothing less.

There’s Something I’ve Never Told You… 🤍

There’s something I’ve never told you… I really want to hug you. I want it so badly. But I know that if I ever hug you, I’ll probably break down and cry. Not because I’m weak, but because I’ve been holding in so many feelings for so long. Just being close to you would be enough to make all those emotions come out. You asked me once, “Surya, are you possessive about me?” Yes, I am. But I always remind myself that my possessiveness should never hurt you or take away your freedom. I never want my feelings to become a burden for you. I just want you to always be the one girl who has a special place in my life. That’s all. Even when I’m possessive, it’s because I care about you deeply—not because I want to control you.
"Always the one girl with a special place in my life... ❤️ — Surya"

You Used to Ask Me, “Why?”… 🌟

You used to ask me, “Why?” Right? Because you’re the only person who has truly stood by me. No matter how angry you get with me, you still check on me and look out for me in your own way. You’ve done so much for me. You may not always say it out loud, but you’ve done things that many people wouldn’t have done. You’ll always be the last girl I’ll truly trust. I don’t know what the future holds, but if there’s one person I’ll always choose, it’s you. I know many people may come into my life, but I don’t want anyone else. That’s my choice. No matter where life takes us, I’ll always choose you. And yes… if you don’t talk to me, it’ll hurt me more than I can explain. I’ll probably be angry, disappointed, and broken for a while. But even then, I won’t hate you, and I won’t stop caring about you. Some people become a part of your life for a while. You became a part of who I am. But you’re always on my mind. No matter what I’m doing, you somehow cross my mind every single day. The reason I always talk to you is because I never want you to feel alone or hurt. I always want to see you happy, confident, and carrying yourself with the same attitude, strength, and smile that make you who you are. I want my Priya to always have high standards!
"No matter where life takes us, I’ll always choose YOU ✨ — Surya"
